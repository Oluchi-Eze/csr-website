Living Joy Book Club

Readers Alliance of CSR — elevating African voices through reading and culturally-aware proofreading.

About the Readers Alliance

We discover that African contextual thinking needs people who understand it in proofreading African writings.

The Living Joy Book Club are readers of books—both already published or in-progress manuscripts that need proofreading for writers. Membership is open for all who want to join. We are flexible on issues and topics related to Black people of African roots, whether in the diaspora or not. Core members are people from Africa and the African diaspora. Join us and become a member. We have a separate forum for non-African descent.

Join the Readers Alliance

How It Works

Read

Choose titles or manuscripts curated by the Alliance, with focus on African contexts and diaspora experiences.

Proofread

Offer culturally-aware feedback that respects context, tone, idioms, and lived realities.

Discuss

Join moderated sessions—by region or theme—to exchange insights and strengthen each work.

Amplify

Support authors by sharing releases, reviews, and community events that celebrate their work.

Core Members

People from Africa and the African diaspora. Participate in all readings, proofreading tracks, and leadership circles.

Open Members
