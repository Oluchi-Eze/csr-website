African Writers Alliance (AWA)

The stories of Africans and black people told by themselves.

About AWA

The African Writers Alliance (AWA) is dedicated to amplifying the voices of Africans and black writers. It provides a platform where stories are written, owned, and told by the people themselves, reflecting their lived experiences and cultural richness.

Purpose

Gathering African writers together to share and interact is the basic purpose of this alliance. We inspire, motivate, and empower each other through collaboration, storytelling, and mentorship.

Membership

Membership is open to Africans who are writers, aspiring writers, or simply passionate about African stories. AWA provides opportunities for networking, workshops, and cross-cultural literary exchange.
